# Grading & condition

Cards are graded on a 1–10 scale (PSA / BGS). Rough value impact relative to a
Near Mint raw card:

- **PSA 10 (Gem Mint):** 3x–10x for chase cards, often more for vintage.
- **PSA 9 (Mint):** ~1.5x–3x.
- **Near Mint (raw):** baseline (1x).
- **Played / Heavily Played:** 0.3x–0.6x.

When quoting a price, state the condition you assumed and use the
`get_card_price` tool's `condition` argument (`mint`, `near_mint`, or `played`)
to adjust the estimate.
